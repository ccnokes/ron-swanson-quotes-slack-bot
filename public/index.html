Ron Swanson Quotes Slack Bot

Click the button below to add the bot to your Slack channel. First you'll be redirected to Slack where you'll authorize the bot, then you'll be redirected to your team's slack.


Usage

Type /ron or /swanson to get a random Ron Swanson quote. You can optionally add a word to match on. For example /ron bacon would return:

"Give me all the bacon and eggs you have."
